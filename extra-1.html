The Great India School Scholarship Test 2022-23

About "The Scholarship Test"

It is talent search & scholarship exam that allows eligible students from class 4th to 9th to study in The Great India School.

Scholarship Test Date - 13 March 2022 (Sunday)

Offline Mode - 1PM - 4PM

Click Here to Register Now

No Examination Fee. Result to be declared during 3rd week of March.

Syllabus : Broadly based on NCERT syllabus of current class of the candidate.

Subject : English, Hindi, Maths, Science, Social Science, Logic & General Knowledge.

Please Note : Exam results & selection of student is strictly upto the management of The Great India School & decision of the school will be considered final.
